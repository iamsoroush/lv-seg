Model Card for BaseLineUnetV2

Model Details

Overview

This model aims to segment left-ventricle in A4C-view echocardiography videos

Documentation

This model will be used as a part of "echoapp service" in AIMedic's image services

Model Parameters

Model Architecture

My model is based on a somehow light-weight UNet-like architecture

Input Format

My model takes a batch of gray-scale images of shape (128, 128, 1) as input

Output Format

My model outputs a batch of binary segmentation map of shape (128, 128, 1) as output

Considerations

Intended Users

  • Any upstream model that needs a binary lv-segmentor for their purposes, such as a lv-volume-estimator

Use Cases

  • Binary segmentation of lv images

Limitations

  • This model can not segment the other parts of the heart such as the right ventricle, atriums, or myocardium.
  • This model can not segment the left ventricle of the heart which works with the battery.
  • This model was only trained on the 4-chamber view of the heart.

Datasets

Echonet-Dynamic

A standard full resting echocardiogram study consists of a series of videos and images visualizing the heart from different angles, positions, image acquisition techniques, and human expert annotations (measurements, tracings, and calculations) to provide a baseline to study the cardiac motion and chamber sizes. The dataset contains 10,030 apical-4-chamber echocardiography videos from individuals who underwent imaging between 2016 and 2018 as part of routine clinical care at Stanford University Hospital. Each video was cropped and masked to remove text and information outside of the scanning sector. The resulting images were then downsampled by cubic interpolation into standardized 112x112 pixel videos.

https://echonet.github.io/dynamic/
